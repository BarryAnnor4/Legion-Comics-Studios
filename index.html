Welcome Legionnaires!

ORIGINAL, NEW, NECESSARY.

Legion Comics Studios is one of the largest and fastest-growing black comic book publishers in Ghana. We focus on telling stories with 60% BIPOC leads, committed to showcasing that black and brown IP is needed, appreciated, and essential.

A powerhouse comic book publishing company, Legion has been taking the world by storm with its thrilling take on mythology and superheroes. Founded by the visionary Bartholomew K.A. Annor in 2017, the company has revolutionized the world of independent comic books.

From its inception, Legion Comics aimed to create a new standard of quality for independent comic books that are centered around minority characters. Their dedication to storytelling, art, and production quality has made them a favorite among comic book enthusiasts.
